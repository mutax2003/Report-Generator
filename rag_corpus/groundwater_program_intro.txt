Ecoventure Inc. designs groundwater monitoring programs for upstream oil and gas facilities in Alberta to support regulatory compliance, reclamation certificate applications, and remedial action plans.

---
Programs typically include monitoring wells screened in targeted hydrostratigraphic units, static water level measurements, and analytical suites aligned with site history (salinity, hydrocarbons, metals).

---
Field data are compared to Alberta Tier 1 soil and groundwater guidelines and site-specific background where applicable. Exceedances are evaluated for temporal trend and spatial distribution before recommending further action.
